Mathematical Optimization and Model Solver
Sept 2020 - Dec 2020

These are MATLAB and MATLAB Live Code files that can solve complex optimization problems using different approach. With input of just the equations to optimize, the codes will output optimized solutions for user selected algorithms.

The models and algorithms include: Bisection method, Newtons Method, Simple Newtons Method, Steepest Descent Method, Modified Newtons Method, Levenberg Marquardt Modification Method, Conjugate Gradient Algorithm, Rank One Correction Method, DFP Algorithm, BFGS Algorithm, Neural Networks, Simplex Iteration Method, Genetic Algorithms (1D and 2D), Randomized Search Method, Simulated Annealing Algorithm, Lagrange Equation Equality Constraints (w/ linear & quadratic), and Positive Definite checker.

They are mostly written in MATLAB Live code to provide user-friendly interface. Some provides visualizations for the convergence of solutions.

Visualization of Nelder-Mead Simplex Method in action. Given multi-order objective function, it searches for the min/max.

Visualization of Golden-section search method, used to find extremum of a multi-ordered function within specified interval.

Snippets of Live code file. Bisection method is used and the processes are displayed every iterations to show how the algorithm/model flows.

Capstone Project - Mechanical Rope Break
Jan 2019 - Apr 2019

MEC 460 Capstone Project where the client, Teck Resources asked our group to design a machine that meets their requirements and improves on the exisiting solution. Teck Resources Ltd. used a rope brake in their open-pit mines to support the shovel cables across two arches over the haul roads. The cables are loaded over the arches using a rope pulley system; once the cables are lifted to the top, a mechanical brake is used to keep tension on the rope.

We actively communicated with the client to identify problems and resolved them with engineering approach.

Traditional method of hauling ropes and wires over roads in the mining site. Major safety concern with 80 reported injuries past 10 years.

After patent and market research, conceptual design solutions were modeled and analyzed with various evaluation criteria.

I was in charge of solid modeling, preparing drawings, BOM and FEA.

Project poster displaying background, objective, solution and etc.
